BetMatchUp Guide

This dashboard visualizes your betting performance, tracks mathematical variance, and utilizes your machine learning models to identify high-value future picks. Here is everything you need to know to maximize your edge.

1. Data Workflow

Load Historical Data

Upload evaluated_bets.json containing resolved matches. This builds your baseline stats, generates charts, and trains the trend-spotter.

Load Pending Data

Upload pending_bets.json. The engine will instantly cross-reference upcoming matches with your historical profit trends.

2. Metric Glossary

EV (Expected Value)

The mathematical value of a bet. Positive EV (+EV) means the bet is profitable long-term, regardless of the immediate result.

Edge (%)

The percentage advantage you hold over the bookmaker's implied probability based on your model's true probability.

ROI (Yield)

Return on Investment. Total profit divided by total stakes. An ROI over 3-5% over a large sample is considered professional.

Unit Value

A standard measure of risk (e.g., 1u = £10). Keeps bankroll management objective. Change your base unit size at the top of the dashboard.

3. Under The Hood: AI & Predictions

Historical Cross-Referencing

Pending matches are dynamically filtered through your historical data. The algorithm identifies if an upcoming bet falls into your historically profitable leagues, specific odds brackets, or high-ROI bet types.

Strong Signal Generation

When an upcoming match possesses a high mathematical EV and perfectly aligns with your most profitable historical trends, the engine automatically flags it as a Strong Signal. These are your premium picks.

4. Reading the Charts (Variance)

The EV vs Actual Chart

This is the most important chart for a data-driven bettor. It plots your Expected Value (Dotted Line) against your Actual Profit (Solid Line).

  • Running Good: If Actual is above EV, you are currently experiencing positive variance (luck). Expect a regression to the mean over time.
  • Running Bad: If Actual is below EV, you are experiencing negative variance (unlucky). Trust the math; if the EV line is pointing up, keep betting.
